Dum Aloo Recipe

Description

This a dish that originates from north india. It uses potatoes and tomato paste to make an extremely rich dish thats full of spices and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 20 baby potatoes, boiled & peeled
  • Oil, for frying
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 pod black cardamom
  • 3 pods green cardamom
  • ½ tsp cloves
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1½ cup tomato puree
  • ¼ cup cashew paste
  • ¼ cup curd
  • 2 cup hot water
  • 2 tbsp coriander, chopped
  • 2 tbsp kasuri methi, crushed
  • ½ tsp garam masala

Steps

  1. Firstly, pressure cook 20 baby potatoes for 1 whistle. Once cooled, peel and prick the potatoes.
  2. In a kadai, heat 4 tbsp oil and fry the potatoes.
  3. Stir occasionally, and fry until the potatoes turn golden brown. Keep aside.
  4. In the same kadai, heat 3 tbsp oil. Add 1 tsp cumin, 2 bay leaves, 1 pod black cardamom, 3 pods green cardamom, and ½ tsp cloves.
  5. Sauté on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  6. Add 1 onion and 1 tsp ginger garlic paste. Sauté until the onion turns golden brown.
  7. Keeping the flame on low, add ½ tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chilli powder, 1 tsp coriander powder, ½ tsp cumin powder, and 1 tsp salt.
  8. Sauté on low flame until the spices turn aromatic.
  9. Add 1½ cup tomato puree and ¼ cup cashew paste (blend 2 tomatoes with 5 cashews to a smooth paste).
  10. Continue cooking until the oil separates from the sides.
  11. Add ¼ cup curd and cook until the oil separates again.
  12. Further, add the fried potatoes and mix gently.
  13. Pour in 2 cups hot water and adjust the consistency as needed.
  14. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  15. After 20 minutes, the oil will float on top and the flavors will be well absorbed by the potatoes.
  16. Add 2 tbsp chopped coriander, 2 tbsp crushed kasuri methi, and ½ tsp garam masala. Mix well.
  17. Finally, enjoy the Dum Aloo Recipe with roti.
